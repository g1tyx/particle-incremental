        A generator gives you n particles every loop. Default is 1 loop per second and 1 particle per loop. 

        The bigger batches upgrade adds 1 to the n (first upgrade makes it 2 particles per loop, 2nd 3 per loop etc). 

        The speed upgrade makes generators do 1 more loop per second (1st upgrade 2loops/s, 2nd upgrade 3l/s etc). 

        The way that the speed upgrade works makes it have diminishing returns. Every upgrade does less that the previous one. 

        

        Manual boost adds n particles to your particles when clicked. 

        The +1/click upgrade increases n by 1. 

        The +1x/click multiplies n by some m (starting at 1) that gets incremented when you buy this upgrade. 

        1st upgrade doubles n, 2nd triples it etc. 

        

        The generator boost multiplies particle production by n for m seconds. 

        

        Every nuclear particle adds a +1x multiplier to both boosts and particle production. 

        (1st particle 2x, 2nd particle 3x etc). 

        

        The buy particle chunk button is self explanatory. 

        The bang button makes an alpha particle from 2 particle chunks. 

        You need at least 1 alpha accelerator to bang. 

        Buying more alpha accelerators multiplies alpha particle gain from the bangs by the total number of accelerators you have.
